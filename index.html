About Us

At Coffee House in Berndorf, Germany, we welcome everyone who shares a love for rich aromas, warm conversations, and thoughtfully crafted cups of coffee. Our beans are sourced from the sun‑kissed highlands of Colombia, one of the world’s leading producers of smooth, high‑quality Arabica coffee. Each batch is roasted with care to highlight its natural sweetness, vibrant notes, and lingering finish. In our cozy and inviting space, guests can relax, connect, and savor each sip at their own pace. From your first visit to your favorite table, every moment here is designed to feel comforting, memorable, and uniquely yours.
